About

ADRIAN GUILLERME
THE ARTIST DEFINING HIMSELF

I am Adrian Guillerme. I am not a Tattoo artist. The human body is my canvas. I live and breath in New York. I draw my inspiration from the works of Escher and H. R. Giger and the streets of my hometown. I learn how to master the art from the master himself Matt Wullenmeg.

During the spring of 2016 I hosted the New York Bad*ss Tattoo convention which drew a large amount of publicity and got over 3,000 attendants on its grand opening. The convention, was held again in 2017 in Chicago with more than 4,000 people attending. A short documentary film was created during the 4 days of the convention and it is available on Vimeo.
